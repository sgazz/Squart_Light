Welcome to Squart

Squart extends Domineering: players alternate placing dominoes while inactive squares block both sides. Play smart to limit your opponent.

  • Horizontal and Vertical players take turns automatically.
  • Inactive squares cannot be covered and appear at the start.
  • Use the sliders to explore different board shapes and inactive ratios.
  • Shortcuts and tooltips help you stay in flow.

How to Play Squart

Squart is a twist on the game Domineering. Players alternate placing dominoes with different orientations while inactive squares act as permanent obstacles on the board.

  1. Turns: The horizontal player (blue) places dominoes along rows; the vertical player (red) places dominoes along columns.
  2. Inactive squares: These “building” tiles cannot be covered and block both players. Adjust their percentage and layout with the slider or let the game generate them randomly.
  3. Objective: The game ends when the player on turn cannot place a valid domino. That player loses and the opponent wins automatically.
  4. Controls: Click an active square to attempt a move. Sliders change board dimensions, and “Regenerate Board” creates a new setup.

Experiment with different board sizes and inactive percentages to discover a balance that suits both players.
